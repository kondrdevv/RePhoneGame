# 📱 Re:Phone Game

**Re:Phone Game** is an open-source economic strategy game where you become a smartphone flipper.  
Buy used phones, repair them, sell at a profit, and build your trading empire.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

- 🛒 **Buy** phones from various markets: Avito, black market, Ozon, AliExpress  
- 🔧 **Repair** via mini-game or hire repair masters  
- 🏷️ **Sell** on your storefront, participate in auctions  
- 📈 **Follow market trends** — prices change in real-time  
- 👥 **Hire staff**: sellers, repairers, managers  
- 🏆 **Open new stores** and expand your network  
- 💰 **Take loans**, buy parts, upgrade skills  
- 🎲 **Play lottery**, collect rare devices

## 🚀 What's New in v4.0

### 1️⃣ Customer Database with History
**Location:** `👥 Customers` tab  
- Every customer now has a **name, age, and profession** (randomly generated).  
- **Purchase history** — what they bought and for how much.  
- **Loyalty system** (0–10) — grows with each purchase.  
- **Word of mouth** — when loyalty > 7, customer has a 10% chance to bring a friend, you get +10 reputation and a notification.

### 2️⃣ Business Plan (Long-Term Goals)
**Location:** `📊 Business Plan` tab  
- Goals with deadlines:  
  - `Earn 500,000 ₽ in 30 days`  
  - `Sell 50 phones in a week`  
- **Progress bar** — shows completion percentage.  
- **Big bonuses** (money + reputation) on completion, with notifications.

### 3️⃣ ReCoin Cryptocurrency
**Location:** `₿ ReCoin` tab  
- New currency with a **floating exchange rate** (updates every 10 seconds).  
- Buy ReCoin with in-game money.  
- Sell ReCoin when the rate goes up — earn profit.  
- **Rate chart** — visualization on canvas.

### 4️⃣ Store Upgrades (Franchise Improvements)
**Location:** `🏢 Franchise` tab  
- 3 upgrade paths per store (levels 0→1→2→…):  
  - 🎨 **Interior Design** — boosts attractiveness (purchase frequency).  
  - 🔒 **Security System** — reduces theft and fine risk.  
  - 📢 **Ad Campaign** — attracts more customers.  
- Each upgrade costs money and has its own level (progress shown).

### 5️⃣ Income Graph
**Location:** Main page (`💰 Balance` card)  
- Line graph showing **capital dynamics** over the last 60 steps (1 step = 1 second).  
- Shows money growth or decline in real time.  
- Visually motivates you to track progress.

### 6️⃣ News Feed (Replaces Old Log)
**Location:** Right panel (replaces the old event log)  
- Beautiful cards with icons:  
  - 📈 — trend (green)  
  - 🚨 — police, theft (red)  
  - 🏆 — achievement (gold)  
  - 💎 — collector's auction (purple)  
  - ✅ — successful sale, repair (green)  
- Each event has a timestamp and type.  
- Feed updates automatically; old events scroll down.

### 7️⃣ New Design (Apple-style + Animations)
**Visual changes:**  
- **Fonts:** Montserrat + -apple-system (Apple-like).  
- **Animations:** smooth transitions, hover transforms, fade effects (cubic-bezier 0.25,0.46,0.45,0.94).  
- **Cards:** lift and glow on hover.  
- **Buttons:** scale on hover with a glow effect.  
- **Stats:** more compact, cleaner.  
- **Overall style:** dark background, neon accents, glassmorphism (blur, transparency).

## 🖥️ Technologies
